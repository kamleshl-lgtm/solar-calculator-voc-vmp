About This Calculator

Calculates the maximum open-circuit voltage (Voc) and minimum operating voltage (Vmp) of a solar PV string, corrected for site temperature extremes — essential for sizing strings within inverter voltage limits.

How to Use
1 Enter the input parameters in the blue (Voc) and green (Vmp) fields.
2 Values are validated instantly and results recalculate as you type.
3 Review the step-by-step breakdown and the final result card.
Key Formulas
Maximum Voc
VOCMax = VOCModule × M + γVoc × (Tmin − TSTC) × M
Minimum Vmp
VmpMin = VmpModule × M + γPmp × (Tcell − TSTC) × M
